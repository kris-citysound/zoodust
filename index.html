ABOUT THE Z

Zoodust is a 90's Party Band based out of Austin, TX that plays a grand majority of top Alternative Rock hits from the 90's including bands such as Third Eye Blind, Fastball, Barenaked Ladies, Oasis, Sugar Ray, Sublime and Weezer (just to name a few).

Zoodust or “The Z” was forged in 2009, by an incredibly music-savvy group of artists; each of which has extensively toured on a national level. After scoping out the local competition, it became apparent that the surrounding Austin area was in need of a more realistic 90's Cover Band. With that knowledge, Craig Dacy, Dean Barry, Adam Hovey and Mike Blackmon decided to set a new standard for cover bands.

They have set out to create a realistic concert experience for the audience. Zoodust puts on a show just like the original bands would have, with no backing-tracks, and one-hundred percent live performance and instruments. They pride themselves in providing excellent crowd interaction, sing-a-longs, and banter that will keep everyone laughing and dancing in a surreal flashback to the not so distant past that is the 90's.
